#-----------------------------
# Changelog
#-----------------------------
- Split the Trade Company regions into the actual regions, rather than by superregion.
- Changed the Shadowlands nations, giving them distinct governments with their own reform tree, and new religions and better reflect the worldbuilding for that area.
- Ghiscari governments now use the prussian militarization mechanic, now called Slave Efficiency.
- Added Religious Sects (Holy Orders) that allow you to assign a 'minor' religion based on the local of the state for specific bonuses. Represents the minor religions different areas would have.
